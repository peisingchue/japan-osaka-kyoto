✈️ Before You Fly — Pack at Home
🛂
Passport Must
Valid 6+ months. Photo backup on your phone.
🎫
Hotel confirmations Must
Rakuten STAY Kyoto (13–16 May) + OneFive Osaka (16–19 May). Screenshot offline.
✈️
Flight tickets (both ways) Must
KIX arrival 09:50 · KIX departure 11:05. Screenshot offline.
💴
Japanese Yen cash Must
At least ¥50,000 (~RM1,500). Many shrines, street food stalls and small restaurants are cash only.
💳
Credit / debit card Must
Notify your bank of overseas travel before flying. Maybank card for hotel discounts.
📶
Japan SIM or Pocket WiFi Must
Pre-order before departure. Essential for maps and translation. IIJmio or Sakura Mobile recommended.
🔋
Power bank Must
Carry-on ONLY. Max 160Wh / 43,000mAh. Max 2 per person. Remove from checked bag!
🔌
Japan plug adapter Must
Japan uses Type A flat 2-pin. Voltage is 100V.
👟
Comfortable walking shoes Must
Broken-in, not new. You'll walk 15,000–20,000 steps daily. No heels.
🩹
Blister plasters Must
You WILL get blisters. Pack 5–6 pads minimum. Non-negotiable.
🧴
Sunscreen SPF50 Must
May sun is strong, especially Arashiyama and Nara Park (outdoors all day).
🌂
Compact umbrella Recommended
May can have surprise rain showers. A small foldable one fits in your day bag.
🧥
Light jacket or cardigan Recommended
For air-conditioned trains and cool evenings. Nights can be breezy.
🎒
Day backpack 15–20L Must
For water, snacks, umbrella and camera daily.
🛍
Foldable tote bag × 2 Recommended
For shopping and airport purchases home. Japan supermarkets charge for plastic bags.
✏️
Pen Recommended
For arrival immigration card. Always have one in your bag.
📱
Download apps before flying Recommended
Google Maps (Japan offline) · Google Translate (Japanese offline pack) · Navitime · Jihanpi · Tabelog · Flush
Day 1 · Arrive → Kyoto
💴
Load Suica IC card at KIX airport
JR counter or vending machine in terminal B1. Load at least ¥10,000.
📋
Fill in Japan arrival card on the plane
Distributed during the flight. Have hotel address ready: Rakuten STAY URBAN Kyoto Shijo Omiya.
Day 2 · Arashiyama
🎟
Sagano Train ticket (pre-booked) Must check
Book online at least 1 week ahead. Screenshot or print your ticket.
🛁
Towel for Tenzan-no-yu Onsen
Bring your own or rent at the onsen for ¥100. Small towel is fine.
Day 3 · Kimono Day
📋
Kimono rental booking confirmation Must
Note the shop address and return time (before 5:30pm).
👡
Slip-on shoes
Much easier to put on and off for kimono. Many temples require removing shoes.
👜
Small bag only
Kimono shops prefer you don't carry a big backpack. Leave the day bag at hotel if possible.
Day 4 · Check Out + Fushimi Inari → Osaka
🧳
Pack all luggage before breakfast
Check out at 10am. Store at Kyoto Station coin locker (¥600) before heading out.
🛁
Towel for Spaworld
Bring your own or rent at Spaworld for ¥100.
💴
¥600 cash for coin locker
Kyoto Station lockers are coin operated. Have exact change ready.
Day 5 · Nara
👕
Don't wear anything nice
Deer WILL grab bags, scarves, anything dangling. Small crossbody or tuck-away bag best.
💴
¥200–400 cash for deer crackers
Shika senbei 鹿せんべい from stalls at park entrance. Buy multiple packs!
Day 6 · Osaka Full Day
🛍
Tote bag for shopping
Don Quijote run today — bring a foldable bag for all your pharmacy and souvenir haul.
🧾
Collect all receipts for tax refund
Any tax-free purchases from the whole trip — bring all to KIX tomorrow for refund.
🧳
Pack bags tonight before onsen
Leave hotel 8am tomorrow. Pack everything tonight so morning is stress-free.

🧾
All tax-free receipts Must
Tax refund counter is before security at KIX. Bring passport + all receipts.

Power bank in carry-on only Must
Confirm this before checking in bags. Max 160Wh / 43,000mAh. Max 2 per person.
